# Esports.Jobs Personas & Journeys

## Persona 1: Emerging Shoutcaster ("Maya")
- **Profile**: Recent college graduate who has built a modest following casting collegiate matches on Twitch. Looking to break into professional broadcast roles while balancing freelance gigs.
- **Goals**: Gain visibility with tournament organizers, build a professional reel, and secure recurring paid casting roles.
- **Pain Points**: Unclear certification pathways, limited insider contacts, and inconsistent gig flow.

### Journey Highlights
1. **Onboarding & Assessment**: Maya completes a skills quiz and uploads highlight clips, unlocking a "Broadcast Specialist" career quest.
2. **Quest Progression**: She follows guided tasks to earn a shoutcasting certification, receives feedback from a mentor, and expands her loadout with verified achievements.
3. **Opportunity Engagement**: Maya receives alerts from the Live Operations Radar, applies to open broadcast roles, and collaborates with other casters in a squad workspace to practice.
4. **Outcome & Retention**: After securing a recurring league contract, Maya participates in seasonal events to mentor newcomers and maintain her presence on the platform.

## Persona 2: Team Operations Manager ("Jordan")
- **Profile**: Former semi-pro player transitioning into back-office operations for a mid-tier esports organization. Responsible for staffing events, managing logistics, and maintaining sponsor relationships.
- **Goals**: Source reliable talent quickly, track team member credentials, and evaluate staffing ROI.
- **Pain Points**: Fragmented tools for scheduling, difficulty vetting freelancers, and limited metrics to report to leadership.

### Journey Highlights
1. **Discovery & Setup**: Jordan configures the Squad Collaboration Hub for the organization's staff, importing existing talent data and defining role requirements.
2. **Talent Sourcing**: Uses the marketplace filters to shortlist candidates with completed loadouts and verified quests, inviting them to seasonal activation events.
3. **Live Event Execution**: Leverages the Live Operations Radar to fill urgent shifts and monitors quest completion to ensure compliance with sponsor deliverables.
4. **Review & Renewal**: Tracks Employer Match Quality metrics and renews sponsorship packages based on transparent performance dashboards.

## Persona 3: Creative Content Producer ("Lena")
- **Profile**: Freelance video editor with a background in esports highlight reels and social content, seeking longer-term collaborations with teams and brands.
- **Goals**: Showcase a portfolio, collaborate with squads on recurring series, and obtain premium clients willing to invest in polished content.
- **Pain Points**: Oversaturated marketplaces, unclear project scopes, and limited feedback loops from clients.

### Journey Highlights
1. **Portfolio Activation**: Lena builds a multimedia loadout, linking motion graphics projects and past brand collaborations, which unlocks advanced creative quests.
2. **Collaboration & Co-Creation**: Joins a cross-functional squad with strategists and social managers to pitch content concepts through the collaboration hub.
3. **Marketplace Engagement**: Applies to curated opportunities, negotiates scope directly within project threads, and earns endorsements that boost her visibility in search.
4. **Growth & Advocacy**: Participates in seasonal workshops, contributes tutorials to the community, and tracks revenue efficiency to prioritize the highest value partnerships.
