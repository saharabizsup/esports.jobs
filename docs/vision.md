# Esports.Jobs Product Vision

## Overview
Esports.Jobs connects aspiring and experienced esports professionals with teams, tournament organizers, content studios, and brands. The platform blends curated opportunities with guided development pathways so players and professionals can grow their careers in a competitive landscape.

## Experience Pillars
- **Career Quests**: Narrative-driven goal tracks that break long-term career aspirations (e.g., becoming an analyst, coach, or production lead) into actionable quests with milestones, resources, and mentors.
- **Loadout Progression**: A gamified skills inventory where members equip certifications, portfolio pieces, and endorsements to visualize readiness for specific roles and unlock advanced opportunities.
- **Squad Collaboration Hub**: Shared workspaces for teams, guilds, or cohorts to coordinate applications, scrims, content drops, and professional development tasks with integrated communication tools.
- **Live Operations Radar**: Real-time feeds surfacing urgent staffing needs for tournaments, broadcasts, or agency projects so vetted talent can respond instantly.
- **Seasonal Events & Rewards**: Time-bound challenges, virtual job fairs, and partner-led activations that reward participation with credibility boosts, exclusive loot, and sponsor connections.

## Core Value Propositions
- **Accelerated Career Discovery**: Tailored role recommendations and quests that clarify paths across competitive, creative, and operational esports domains.
- **Proof of Skill & Experience**: Verified credentials, match history analytics, and peer endorsements presented in a portable career loadout.
- **Trusted Marketplace**: Curated, vetted opportunities with transparent compensation, requirements, and success stories to reduce risk for both talent and employers.
- **Community-Led Growth**: Collaborative hubs, mentorship programs, and seasonal events that nurture long-term engagement and network effects.

## Success Metrics
- **Talent Activation Rate**: Percentage of new members who complete their first career quest and apply to at least one opportunity within 30 days.
- **Employer Match Quality**: Post-placement satisfaction scores and repeat posting rate from hiring partners.
- **Loadout Completion Velocity**: Average time for members to fill critical skill slots or obtain recommended certifications for target roles.
- **Community Retention**: Monthly active squads and cohort participation rates in seasonal events.
- **Revenue Efficiency**: Ratio of marketplace transaction volume and sponsorship revenue to acquisition costs.
