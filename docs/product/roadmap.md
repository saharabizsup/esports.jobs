# Product Roadmap

## Vision Statement
Build the destination platform that connects esports talent with teams, tournament organizers, and industry employers through transparent opportunities and community-driven insights.

## Current Stage
- **Status:** Pre-MVP discovery
- **Focus:** Validating core user journeys for talent and hiring managers

## MVP Definition
- Curated job listings sourced from verified partners
- Player and staff profiles with portfolio, match history, and availability
- Application tracking for teams and organizations
- Admin dashboard for content moderation and partner onboarding
- Email notifications for new matches between talent and opportunities

## Roadmap Overview

### Now (0-3 months)
- Finalize user personas and key journeys
- Implement MVP feature set
- Establish beta partner program and content pipeline
- Integrate analytics and user feedback loops

### Next (3-6 months)
- Launch public beta with invite system
- Add advanced search filters and saved searches
- Introduce team collaboration tools for recruiting squads
- Expand international coverage and localization

### Later (6-12 months)
- Monetization experiments (premium placements, subscription tiers)
- Mobile-responsive enhancements and native app exploration
- Partnerships with tournament platforms for automated result ingestion
- Community features: endorsements, portfolio highlights, success stories

## Key Metrics
- Weekly active talent and recruiters
- Job application conversion rate
- Time-to-hire for partner organizations
- Net promoter score (NPS) from both sides of the marketplace

## Risks & Mitigations
- **Risk:** Supply-demand imbalance. **Mitigation:** Controlled partner intake and targeted talent acquisition.
- **Risk:** Data quality of job postings. **Mitigation:** Verification workflows and partner SLAs.
- **Risk:** User trust and safety. **Mitigation:** Moderation tooling, reporting mechanisms, and code of conduct.

## Review Cadence
- Monthly product review syncing stakeholder feedback and metrics.
- Quarterly roadmap refresh based on validated learnings.
